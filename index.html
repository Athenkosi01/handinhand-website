The systems we have now for organ donation are not very good, they are hard to use. Do not always tell people what is going on. This means that people who need transplants might not get them on time. Our website, Hand in Hand wants to make things better. It will help people who want to donate organs, people who need transplants and doctors talk to each other and work together. Hand in Hand is really important because it will help people who need organ transplants and people who want to be organ donors.

What is Hand-In-Hand?

Hand-In-Hand is a platform designed to help patients who need organ transplants connect with potential donors in a safe, transparent and accessible way. The aim of this platform is to reduce long waiting lists for organ transplants and improve access to life-saving medical care.

The platform also helps reduce corruption that sometimes occurs in organ donation systems such as illegal organ trading and unfair prioritisation of wealthy patients. Hand-In-Hand promotes transparency and ensures ethical organ allocation based on medical need.

What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation occurs when a person donates one or more organs to help another person survive or improve their quality of life. Donating organs is a selfless act that saves lives and gives patients a second chance.

Why Do People Need Organ Donations?

Organ donation becomes necessary when organs stop functioning properly due to diseases or severe medical conditions.

  • Kidney failure may require a kidney transplant.
  • Liver diseases such as cirrhosis may require a liver transplant.
  • Severe heart conditions may require a heart transplant.
  • Serious lung diseases may require a lung transplant.

Misunderstanding About Organ Donations

Donors tend to fear that there may be some mistakes during operation and then doctors fail to save them leading them to death. They also fear that they may get some illness(side effects )after donation that can not be prevented, but we assure you that there will be no possible mistakes or you will be in warm hands. Some people say that organ donation is against their religious beliefs .It is important to understand the advantages and disadvantages of donating before donating to prevent confusion or misunderstanding .
